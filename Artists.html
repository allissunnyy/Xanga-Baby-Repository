Artists of the Impressionist Movement

Impressionism was a groundbreaking 19th‑century art movement that changed how we see the world. Originating in France, artists like Claude Monet, Pierre‑Auguste Renoir, and Edgar Degas captured fleeting moments of modern life, focusing on light and movement rather than strict realism.
