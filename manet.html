Édouard Manet

Édouard Manet (1832–1883)

Édouard Manet bridged Realism and Impressionism, challenging traditional art with bold subjects and techniques. Though slightly older than core Impressionists, his modern themes and loose brushwork inspired the movement.

Key Works

Legacy

Manet's flat colors, strong outlines, and contemporary subjects paved the way for Impressionism while maintaining a unique modernist edge.
