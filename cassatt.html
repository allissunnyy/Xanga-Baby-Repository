Mary Cassatt

Mary Cassatt (1844–1926)

The American Impressionist in Paris, Cassatt specialized in tender mother-child relationships and women's private worlds with clean lines and bright color.

Key Works

Legacy

Cassatt introduced Impressionism to America and championed women artists internationally.
